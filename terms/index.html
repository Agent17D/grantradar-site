2. Description of Service

GrantSignal is a weekly email newsletter that curates federal grant opportunities from public government sources. Each week, we monitor newly posted grants, summarize them, assign AI-generated relevance scores (1–5 stars), and deliver a digest to your inbox every Tuesday morning.

Our goal is to save nonprofits, schools, and community organizations the time it takes to manually search federal grant databases. We do not write grants, submit applications, or represent you to any federal agency.

3. Free and Paid Subscriptions

Free tier: Subscribers on the free plan receive a limited weekly digest featuring the top 3 matched grant opportunities, urgent deadline alerts, and direct links to Grants.gov listings. The free tier is available indefinitely — no credit card required.

Basic ($29/month): Includes the full weekly digest with all matched grants (typically 20–50 per week), AI fit scores, and mission-area personalization.

Premium ($49/month): Includes everything in Basic, plus geography and budget filtering, Thursday deadline reminder emails, SAM.gov expiry alerts, and up to 3 team seats.

Features and pricing may change with notice. Current pricing is always displayed at grantsignal.news.
